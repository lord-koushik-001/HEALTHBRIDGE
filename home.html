Who We Are

Built by People
Who Care

HealthBridge started in 2023 as a volunteer initiative in Chennai — three friends, a borrowed van, and a vision to bring doctors to the people. Today we run 20+ camps across South India.

Every camp is free. Every doctor is certified. Every decision is made with the patient first.

Questions

Everything you need to know before registering for a camp.

Ask Us →
Is registration completely free?
+

Yes — 100% free. All medical camps listed on HealthBridge are free of charge. There are no hidden fees, consultation charges, or medication costs at the camp.

Who can register for a camp?
+

Anyone can register — there are no age or income restrictions. All camps are open to the general public. Some camps may have specific eligibility (e.g. blood donation requires age 18–65).

Do I need to bring documents?
+

No documents are required to attend. Just show up at the camp location on the scheduled date. Your registration confirmation is sufficient.

Can I register for multiple camps?
+

Yes, you can submit a separate registration for each camp you'd like to attend. Each registration is independent.

How will I be notified of my registration?
+

You'll see a confirmation page immediately after registering. Keep your registration details handy — we recommend noting the camp name, date, and location.
