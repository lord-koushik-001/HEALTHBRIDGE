At the Camp

What to
Expect

01

Walk-In Welcome

No appointment needed on the day. Show up, check in at the registration desk, and you'll be guided to the right station.

02

Health Screening

Certified medical staff conduct your checkup in a clean, private setting. Results shared with you on the spot.

03

Doctor Consultation

Every participant gets 1-on-1 time with a qualified doctor to discuss findings and ask questions.

04

Free Medicines

Basic medications and prescriptions provided free of charge based on the doctor's assessment.

05

Health Report

Leave with a written health summary to keep for your records or share with your family physician.

06

Follow-Up Support

Our team will connect you with nearby clinics or arrange a doctor call within 72 hours if needed.
